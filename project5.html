project of furnishing SAS Hotel room as interpretation of "fluid & postindustrial"

The topic which I got was "fluid". Word "fluid" brings me on mind mixing colours, liquids and rounded shapes. Therefore I designeds room in which those main ideas are visible. Floor is divided into three different colours what is showing areas of this room. Shapes of ceiling and tv wall could brings on our minds ocean waves. I have chosen also special furniture and natural materials what is compatible with postindustrial conception.
